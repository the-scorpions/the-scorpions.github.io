Who Are We ?

We are a team of Three Second Year Computer Science Students. We are Developers, Problem Solvers and Coders. We love building cool things using our ability to code. We hope to make the world a better place through our code! We are a diverse team consisting of Hardware as well as Software Minds. We are huge nerds and we love learning new Technologies and exploring the unknowns.

Why This Community ?

This community was started with one single Aim : To Help Young Developers. We wish to guide others by sharing our knowledge and experience. We faced a lot of difficulty regarding the "How and Where to Begin ?". We spent nearly 6 months trying to explore various aspects of CS. And as such we decided to start this community so that other Newcomers don't have to spend 6 months trying to gather all such information. We are just common everyday Nerds making an effort to help those like Us.

Plans For This Community

We have big plans for Growing this Community! We wish to organise Workshops and Tutorials on all things Computer Science and hope to share the knowledege we have gathered over the past Year.
