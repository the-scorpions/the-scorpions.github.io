What Are Hackathons?

Many people believe Hackathons are places where people come to Hack Stuff (The Actual Act of Hacking a Device). And that is a complete myth. Hackathons are events or competetions where people gather to build cool stuff whether it be hardware or software. Hackathons are supposed to test your ability to work flexibly with any framework and Build Things that solve some real life problem. Moreover, If you're an Entrepreneur, You'll love Hackathons. Hackathons are not just about Coding, It also involves Pitching your Solution as well as Developing a Business Plan around that Solution. Hackathons are a great place for Kick starting a Start Up. Moreover, The Cool free Swags, Food and T-shirts more than make up for the Effort required!

Should You Participate in Hackathons ?

YES! Especially if you are interested in becoming a Software Developer. Hackathons give you a small taste of what it might be like to Start your own Company. From Coding to Pitching, You'll Experience it all in a Hackathon!

How To Prepare For A Hackathon?

Hackthons require its participants to form teams and develop solutions from Scratch i.e No Pre-built Code is allowed! Hackathons run anywhere from 24 hours to 72+ Hours Consecutively, So Make Sure you get some proper Sleep Before Hand!

Form your team before hand! Having a Good Team is essential in making sure you perform your best. After all Hackathons are team based Competetions!
