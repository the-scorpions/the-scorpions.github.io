Workshop Details

We are pleased to announce our first Workshop. This Workshop is aimed towards Beginners and Newbies entering the world of computer science. This Workshop will include a Deep intro to various aspects of Computer Science along with A Fun Game of Kahoot at the end!

Date : 1st September, 2019
Location : TBA

NOTE : We are Not Associated With Any College, Firm or Institute. The Workshop is Self Sponsored by the Organizing Team. We Donot Provide Any Travel or Food Facilities, The Participants have to arrange their Travel at their Risk on their Expenses.

Topics Covered

Web Development

Kick Start your web Development lives and take a deeper dive into both Front-End and Back-End Web Development along with a intro to Hosting and Databases.

App Development

Get an intro to Android, iOS and Hybrid App Development. Explore various Frameworks and Tools involved in App Development.

Embedded Systems

Explore the world of Embedded Systems. Get an intro to Various Micro-Controllers and Micro-Processors like Arduino and Raspberry PI.

UI/UX

Learn about various tools and Frameworks involved in designing a fluid, responsive and Scalable UI with Optimum UX.

Cyber Security

Learn about some basic Fundamentals of Computer Networks and Cyber Security. We think we are safe and Secure, But is it so ?

QnA

Get a Chance to interact with our Speakers and clear any doubts you may have regarding any aspect of Computer Science.
